Whoami $$\bar{x} := \bigg(\prod_{i=0}^N x_i\bigg)^{1 / N}$$ $$\log \bar{x} = \frac{1}{N} \bigg(\sum_{i=0}^N \log x_i\bigg),$$ i.e., the logarithm of the geometric mean of a variable is given by the arithmetic mean of the logarithm of the variable.
